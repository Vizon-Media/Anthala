Anthala, established in 2025, is a dynamic company specializing in construction, renovations, and interior design. We take pride in our innovative approach and attention to detail in every project we undertake.

Experience

With over 10 years of activity in the field, we have managed to build a solid reputation based on professionalism and quality results. We have successfully completed over 50 projects of various complexities.

Team

Our team consists of over 10 dedicated specialists, including architects, interior designers, craftsmen, and qualified technicians. Each team member brings valuable experience and expertise in the construction field.

We collaborate with an extensive network of over 20 trusted suppliers and partners, which allows us to offer materials of the highest quality at competitive prices. We are official partners of the top brands in the industry.
